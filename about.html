About the Game

Welcome to the Fruit Cutting Game! This fun and interactive game challenges your reflexes and accuracy as you slice through various falling fruits.

Objective:

Your goal is to cut as many fruits as possible while avoiding bombs that will end your game if touched. Stay sharp and quick!

How to Play:

  • Move your mouse to aim and click on the falling fruits to slice them.
  • Each fruit you cut adds to your score, while bombs will end your game if clicked.
  • If more than two fruits fell before being cut, the game will get over.
